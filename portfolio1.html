Transparency Design for AliPay

The original Alipay sign-up page was quite plain, offering only a single link to all the terms and conditions. That link led to a text-heavy webpage, which made it difficult for users to understand how their personal information would be used.

Goal

I redesigned Alipay’s registration interface, simplifying the dense legal terms into a clear and user-friendly format that helps users easily understand what types of personal information the app collects.
